🆘 Complete Emergency Survival Guide

Know what to do in any emergency situation

🔥 FIRE EMERGENCY

  • STOP, DROP, ROLL if clothes catch fire
  • Feel doors with back of hand before opening
  • Stay below smoke level - crawl low
  • Use stairs, NEVER elevators
  • Cover nose/mouth with wet cloth
  • Close all doors behind you
  • Meet at designated assembly point
  • Never re-enter a burning building

🚑 MEDICAL EMERGENCY

  • Call 911 immediately
  • Don't move unconscious person
  • Apply firm pressure to bleeding wounds
  • For choking: Perform Heimlich maneuver
  • Keep person warm and comfortable
  • Have medical info ready (allergies, medications)
  • Perform CPR if trained and needed
  • Stay calm and reassure the person

⚠️ ACTIVE THREAT

  • RUN, HIDE, FIGHT (in that order)
  • Lock and barricade doors
  • Turn off lights, stay quiet
  • Stay away from windows
  • Silence your phone
  • Don't open door for anyone
  • Wait for police instructions
  • Only fight as last resort

🌊 FLOOD / WATER DAMAGE

  • Move to higher ground immediately
  • Avoid walking through flood water
  • Turn off electricity if safe
  • Don't use elevators
  • Protect important documents
  • Listen for emergency alerts
  • Don't drive through flooded areas
  • Watch for contaminated water

💨 GAS LEAK

  • DON'T use any switches or flames
  • Open windows immediately
  • Leave the area quickly
  • Don't use phones inside
  • Call gas company from outside
  • Don't re-enter until cleared
  • Don't turn lights on/off
  • Evacuate everyone immediately

💡 POWER OUTAGE

  • Use flashlights, NOT candles
  • Unplug sensitive electronics
  • Keep fridge/freezer closed
  • Check on neighbors
  • Stay away from downed wires
  • Have emergency kit ready
  • Keep one light switched on
  • Don't use generators indoors

🌪️ EARTHQUAKE / NATURAL DISASTER

  • Drop, Cover, Hold On
  • Move to interior room, no windows
  • Stay away from glass
  • Listen to official instructions
  • Have emergency kit with water/food
  • Stay indoors until shaking stops
  • Watch for falling objects
  • After: check for gas leaks

🩹 FIRST AID BASICS

  • CPR: 30 compressions, 2 breaths
  • For burns: cool water, DON'T use ice
  • For bleeding: elevate, apply pressure
  • For shock: lay down, elevate feet
  • Keep first aid kit accessible
  • Learn Heimlich maneuver
  • Know allergy emergency response
  • Have emergency numbers saved
